## How to analyze your data
In your dashboard, you have everything you need at your disposal.

    ### Details
    We provide a way for you to track your revenue, order count, average order value, tickets & review count. (Which you can customize and select the range of time you'd like to see)

    ### Store Visits
    Shows more insight on the countries that are accessing your store. (This data is only temporary and deleted after 24 hours)

    ### Customer Retention
    Within the selected range selected in details, out of all users created on checkout, how many in percentage actually completed their order.

    ### Recent orders
    Shows you an overview of your last 5 completed orders
