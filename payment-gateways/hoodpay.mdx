## How to link Hoodpay

* Login to your account and go to Settings > Developer
* Copy your Business ID and Webhook Secret.
* Generate a new key for "API Keys", set it to any name (ex. Sellburst) and set it to "No expiration"
* Press on top of the key and copy it.

You're finally finished.
