## How to link Square

* To get the information required on the settings, go [here](https://developer.squareup.com/console/en/apps)
* Create a developer application
* After creating, press "Production" in the tab at the top and save your Application Id (App Id) and the subsequent Access Token
* Once that is done, press "Locations" on the navbar to your left. Confirm that it's still in production mode and save your Location ID

You're finally finished.
