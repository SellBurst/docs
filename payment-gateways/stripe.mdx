## How to link Stripe

* Go to your dashboard, press settings on the bottom left. In the next page, press payments.
* After being in the payments page, press on stripe and we will redirect you to their website.
* After the onboarding process is complete, you will be redirected back and your stripe will then be available to be used in your store!
